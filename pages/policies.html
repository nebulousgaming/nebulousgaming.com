2. Privacy Policy

We take player and developer data data privacy seriously. This policy outlines how we handle data logged during your network interactions:

  • 2.1

    Information Collection: We collect nickname identifiers, public IP addresses (to check node routing pings), and email details provided voluntarily through support tickets.

  • 2.2

    Data Protection: We do not sell or leak player metrics to third parties. All operational database records are securely hosted on enterprise-grade cloud backend structures.

  • 2.3

    Session Logging: Server traffic logs are retained for a maximum of 30 days to mitigate network attacks and maintain structural performance safety, after which they are purged.
