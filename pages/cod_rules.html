Call of Duty 4 Server Rules

Please adhere to the following rules to maintain a fair, clean, and competitive gaming environment.

  • #0

    Don't Use Any Kind Of Hacks/Cheats Or Will Be Permanently Banned from the Server.

  • #1

    Using Glitches/Elevator will result in kick followed by temporary ban.

  • #2

    Player skins are not supported and will lead to ban. Custom weapon skins are allowed.

  • #3

    No arguing with admins. (Be Nice or Leave)

  • #4

    No Abusive Language Or Bad behavior Towards Any Admins Or Other players.

  • #5

    No offensive or potentially offensive names, annoying names, or in-game color in names.

  • #6

    No recruiting for your clan, your server, or anything else.

  • #7

    No advertising or spamming of websites or servers.

  • #8

    No profanity or offensive language. (in any language)

  • #9

    Offense players must play for the objective and support their team.
